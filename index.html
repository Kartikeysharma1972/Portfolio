FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What services do you offer? +
I specialize in AI agent development using LangGraph, full-stack AI applications (React + FastAPI), RAG and knowledge systems, workflow automation, prompt engineering, and ML/data analysis. If it involves making AI reliable in production, that's my zone.
How much do you charge? +
Pricing depends on project scope. I offer hourly rates ($30–60/hr) or fixed-price project quotes. Most small-to-medium AI agent projects fall between $500–$2000. Email me and I'll send a detailed quote within 24 hours.
What does your development process look like? +
Discovery call → architecture design → iterative build with weekly demos → production delivery with Docker, docs, and deployment support. You always see progress, no surprises.
Can you work with my existing tech stack? +
Yes. I build standalone AI agents that integrate via APIs, or I work within your current stack. My FastAPI backends are modular and designed to plug into existing infrastructure easily.
Do you sign NDAs / keep projects confidential? +
Yes. I can sign an NDA, work under confidentiality clauses, and follow your security requirements. If needed, I can also work inside your GitHub org with limited access.
Do you provide post-launch support? +
Yes. I offer a handover with documentation, monitoring guidance, and a support window for bug fixes. Ongoing maintenance and feature iterations are available as a monthly retainer.
What do you need from me to get started? +
A quick brief (goals, users, success metrics), any existing docs/APIs, and sample inputs/outputs. After a short discovery call, I’ll share a clear scope, timeline, and quote.
How long does a project typically take? +
Simple AI integrations: 1–2 weeks. Single agent tools: 2–3 weeks. Multi-agent systems or full-stack apps: 3–6 weeks. I give accurate timelines upfront and I hit them.
